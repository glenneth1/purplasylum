Personal favourites

My personal list of favourite artists and albums from over the too many years. In no particular order.

  • Radiohead - Hail to the Thief - A masterful blend of Radiohead's raw emotion and experimental edge, creating an album that feels both hauntingly prescient and deeply personal.
  • Depeche Mode - Depeche Mode's moody synth-pop and dark, introspective lyrics have redefined electronic music and influenced countless artists, making them a timeless favorite.
  • Sharon Van Etten - A Voice of Strength in Vulnerability.
  • Damien Rice - O - Haunting, emotional, love and heartbreak
